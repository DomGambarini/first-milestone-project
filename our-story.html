Our Story

Welcome to Hugo's Bistro, where every dish tells a tale of culinary excellence and heartfelt passion. Nestled in the heart of Cardiff, our French restaurant has been a cherished destination for gastronomic connoisseurs since 1985.

Hugo Dupont, the visionary behind this charming bistro, inherited his love for French cuisine from his grandmother, who spent her days perfecting traditional family recipes. Inspired by her culinary prowess, Hugo embarked on a lifelong journey to bring authentic flavors and a touch of his own creativity to the plates of Parisians and international guests alike.

At Hugo's Bistro, we believe in celebrating the vibrant traditions of French cooking while infusing them with a modern twist. Our team of talented chefs meticulously source the finest ingredients from local markets, ensuring that each creation embodies the essence of seasonal produce and artisanal craftsmanship.

Step inside our warm and inviting space, adorned with vintage French posters and the soft melody of classic chansons. Allow us to transport you to the enchanting world of French gastronomy, where every bite unravels a story of dedication, innovation, and a deep-rooted love for the culinary arts.
