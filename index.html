Our Story

Welcome to Hugo's Bistro, where every dish tells a tale of culinary excellence and heartfelt passion. Nestled in the heart of Paris, our French restaurant has been a cherished destination for gastronomic connoisseurs since 1985. Hugo Dupont, the visionary behind this charming bistro, inherited his love for French cuisine from his grandmother, who spent her days perfecting traditional family recipes. Inspired by her culinary prowess, Hugo embarked on a lifelong journey to bring authentic flavors and a touch of his own creativity to the plates of Parisians and international guests alike.
